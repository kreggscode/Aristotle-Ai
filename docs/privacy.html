Last Updated: October 18, 2025

At Aristotle AI, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Aristotle AI is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Favorites Data: Your saved favorite quotes are stored locally on your device
  • App Preferences: Settings like dark mode, notification preferences, and selected categories
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about app crashes and performance to improve the app

2. Information We Don't Collect

We respect your privacy and do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Any data that can personally identify you

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the app's functionality
  • Remember your preferences and favorites
  • Send daily quote notifications (if enabled)
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance

4. Data Storage

All your data (favorites, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal data on external servers. Your information stays with you.

5. Third-Party Services

Aristotle AI may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates
  • Android WorkManager: For scheduling daily notifications

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

  • Google Privacy Policy

6. Notifications

If you enable daily quote notifications, we use Android's notification system to deliver a daily quote at your preferred time (default: 9 AM). You can disable this at any time in the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Aristotle AI is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Since data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. We recommend keeping your device secure with a password or biometric lock.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (all stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (uninstall the app or clear app data)
  • Disable notifications at any time
  • Clear cache from the app settings

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. International Users

Aristotle AI can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no data is transferred internationally.
